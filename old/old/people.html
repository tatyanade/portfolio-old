Coded through OpenSCAD and printed through the MakerBot Replicator 2.

 

This is a construction kit with printable, parametric limbs and body parts. All the joint slots are the same size which allows construction of structures. All forms are constructed through additive and subtractive geometric shapes to form a human-like figure.

 

 

process

 

I started off by prototyping a ball and socket joint. I went through a few different 3d printing prototypes until I got a design that worked well: The end socket covers more than half of the ball, but has slits that allow it to expand when being fitted. It also has a cut out of a sphere which allows joints to be rotated to almost 90 degrees. This allows the use of a double joint (used in the elbows, shoulders, knees, and hips) to achieve more realistic mobility.
