Allochory

 

Collaborative project with Sydney Ayers, Annie Huang, and Matthew Boffenkamp created as final for Experimental Game Design.

 

Inspired by Risk, we created a game about weeds, and how they spread. Players try to take over as much land as possible by having their weeds grow in as many plots as possible.
