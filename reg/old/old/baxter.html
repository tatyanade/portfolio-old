feeding with baxter

 

Collaborative project with Kearnie Lin, Lingdong Huang, and Vicki Long.

 

Throughout the course of the semester, we worked with a Baxter robot, using machine vision, facial recognition, and object recognition make baxter feed people based on facial cues.

 

Done under Professor Akihiko Yamaguchi for the class Humanoids, at the Robotics Department in Carnegie Mellon University.
